About

Complex systems.
Zero compromises.

I build ML systems that make the impossible inevitable. Whether it's compressing 24‑minute simulations into 12 ms inference or teaching language models to monitor their own cognition—I transform theoretical breakthroughs into production reality.

Performance

Speed without compromise

Neural emulation delivering 125,000× speedups at 99.8% accuracy. Systems scale from laptops to cloud, processing billions of data points for governments & policy‑makers.

Cost & Sustainability

Impact at scale

By replacing hour-scale simulations with millisecond neural surrogates, I’ve cut compute energy by ≈39–47 MWh per 1M scenarios on workstations and ≈275–307 MWh per 1M in datacentres (PUE-adjusted), worth ≈£10–12k and ≈£60–81k in electricity-only savings, respectively.
