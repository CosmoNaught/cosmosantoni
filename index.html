About

Complex systems.
Zero compromises.

I build ML systems that make the impossible inevitable. Whether it's compressing 24-minute simulations into 12ms inference or teaching language models to monitor their own cognition—I transform theoretical breakthroughs into production reality.

Speed without compromise. I've achieved 125,000× speedups through neural emulation while maintaining 99.8% accuracy. My systems scale from laptops to cloud infrastructure, processing billions of data points for international governments & policy-makers.

I don't just publish papers. I ship production code. From mathematical theory at Imperial College London to deployed systems saving lives at scale. End-to-end: models, APIs, deployment, and yes—even the websites.

Recognition through impact. SAGE Award from UK Government's Chief Scientific Officers. Publications in The Lancet and Nature. Speaker at WHO, MSF, and data.org. But the real validation? Eight production systems deployed globally, processing millions of scenarios daily.

Full-stack, full-speed. PyTorch for neural architectures. CUDA for GPU optimization. DuckDB for billion-row analytics. React for real-time visualizations. Whether it's metacognitive AI or epidemiological forecasting—my philosophy is simple: make it faster, make it smarter, make it scale.
